✈️ AVIATOR – Crash Game Guide

🎮 How to Play

Objective: Cash out before the plane crashes to multiply your bet!

📍 Game Interface

  • Multiplier Graph: Shows the rising multiplier (starts at 1x) as the plane ascends.
  • Bet Amount: Set your wager before the round starts.
  • Auto Cash-Out: Pre-set a multiplier to automatically cash out (e.g., 2x).
  • Manual Cash-Out: Click "Cash Out" anytime to lock in profits.
  • Round History: Displays past multipliers (e.g., 1.21x, 5.8x, CRASH).

💰 Payout Rules

  • ✅ If you cash out BEFORE the crash: Payout = Bet × Multiplier (e.g., $10 × 3x = $30).
  • ❌ If you don’t cash out in time: You lose your entire bet when the plane crashes.

🎮 Game Actions

Action Description
Place Bet Set your wager before takeoff.
Auto Cash-Out Pre-select a multiplier to cash out automatically.
Manual Cash-Out Click to cash out mid-flight (risk/reward!).
Double Bet Quickly rebet the same amount.
History Check past rounds for patterns (no real strategy).

📊 Strategy Tips

  • Low Risk: Cash out early (e.g., 1.5x–2x) for frequent small wins.
  • High Risk: Aim for 5x+ multipliers (less frequent, higher rewards).
  • Important: Never chase losses – the crash is random!

🧠 Key Rules

  • The crash multiplier is random – no predictable pattern.
  • House Edge: Typically 1–5% (e.g., crashes at 1x–100x+).
  • Multiplayer: Compete against others to see who cashes out highest.

✈️ Pro Tip: Use auto cash-out to avoid emotional decisions!

🎮 Example Round

  • Bet $20, set auto cash-out at 3x.
  • Plane takes off – multiplier rises: 1x → 2x → 3x (auto cash-out!).
  • You win $60 ($20 × 3x).
  • Plane crashes at 4.7x – late players lose.
